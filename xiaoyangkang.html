Xiaoyang Kang

I am currently a Research Engineer at a video generation startup. Previously, I spent several productive years at Alibaba Group, where I served as a Research Engineer at Alimama Creative Lab, Tongyi Lab, and DAMO Academy. My work primarily focused on pushing the boundaries of generative AI and its industrial applications.

I obtained my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). My research interests lie in Generative Models, Video Generation, and Enhancement. I am passionate about building next-generation creative tools powered by foundation models.
